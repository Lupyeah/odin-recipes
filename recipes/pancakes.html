Pancakes

Fluffy, golden pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch. Serve them with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar.

Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
Steps:
  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter; whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side.
